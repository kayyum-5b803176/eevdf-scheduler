• EevdfApp.kt was replaced by SchedulerApplication.kt (@HiltAndroidApp).

  after deleting EevdfApp.kt the build is consistent.
• Full rationale, dependency graph, remaining manual deps and risk
  assessment are in HILT_MIGRATION.md (included).
• A full Gradle/AGP compile (Android SDK + Hilt/KSP) should be run in
  Android Studio / CI to generate the DI code.
